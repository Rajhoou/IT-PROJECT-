Vedanta vs. Nagarjuna: Non-Duality in Debate

Vedanta asserts the ultimate reality of Brahman, an unchanging, infinite consciousness. Nagarjuna, on the other hand, dismantles all fixed concepts, arguing that true wisdom lies in realizing emptiness (Śūnyatā). Both philosophies attempt to transcend duality—one through affirmation, the other through negation.
